Legal Notice & Privacy Policy

Website: aventur.biz
Contact: legal@aventur.biz
Last updated: 14 May 2026

This page explains how this website handles privacy, contact requests, scheduling, and analytics.

This website is operated as a private website. For privacy and security reasons, the private residential address of the website owner/operator is not published here.

For legal, privacy, GDPR, or data protection requests, contact: legal@aventur.biz

Where domain-operator verification is required, you may also consult the public WHOIS/RDAP record for aventur.biz and use the registrar-provided contact mechanisms where available.

1. Who Is Responsible for This Website

The data controller for this website is: The owner/operator of aventur.biz. Contact: legal@aventur.biz.

This contact address can be used for privacy questions, GDPR requests, legal notices, and data protection matters.

2. What Data This Website May Process

This website may process limited personal data when you visit the website, contact us, book a meeting, or agree to analytics cookies.

This may include:

truncated or anonymised IP-related information;
country of origin;
browser and device information;
pages visited;
time and date of visit;
cookie or consent status;
information you send by email;
information you provide when booking a meeting through Calendly.

The website does not seek to identify visitors from technical visit data, and full IP addresses are not used for visitor profiling.

3. Contact by Email

If you contact legal@aventur.biz, your email address, message content, and any information you include may be processed so we can read, respond to, and keep appropriate records of the communication.

Please do not send sensitive personal information unless it is necessary.

4. Meeting Scheduling with Calendly

This website may use Calendly to allow visitors to schedule meetings.

If you book a meeting, Calendly may process information such as your name, email address, selected meeting time, timezone, answers to booking questions, and any notes you provide.

Calendly may process this information as a service provider for scheduling and may also process some data under its own privacy terms. You should review Calendly’s privacy information before using the scheduling tool.

5. Google Analytics 4

This website may use Google Analytics 4 to understand how the website is used and how it can be improved.

By agreeing to analytics cookies, you help us understand how this website is used, which pages are useful, and how the website can be improved.

The analytics cookie is a first-party cookie set for this website. It is used only for website analytics, such as measuring visits, sessions, page views, and aggregated usage patterns.

It is not used by this website for advertising, ads retargeting, cross-site tracking, or building advertising profiles.

This website does not use Google Analytics advertising features, Google Ads remarketing, or audience-based advertising through Google Analytics.

Where analytics consent is denied, analytics cookies are not used.

6. Cookies

This website may use:

essential cookies needed for the website to work;
consent cookies to remember your privacy choices;
optional analytics cookies, if you agree to them;
cookies or similar technologies from Calendly when using the scheduling feature.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and, where available, through the website’s cookie settings.

7. Why Data Is Processed

Personal data may be processed for the following reasons:

to operate and secure the website;
to respond to messages;
to schedule meetings;
to understand and improve website performance;
to comply with legal obligations;
to prevent abuse or misuse of the website.

Depending on the situation, the legal basis may be consent, legitimate interest, steps before entering into a possible agreement, or legal obligation.

8. Sharing Data

Personal data may be processed by service providers used to operate this website, including:

website hosting providers;
email providers;
Calendly, for meeting scheduling;
Google Analytics 4, for analytics where enabled.

We do not sell personal data.

9. How Long Data Is Kept

Personal data is kept only for as long as necessary.

Typical retention periods are:

website security or diagnostic logs: usually up to 12 months;
emails: as long as needed to respond and keep appropriate records;
Calendly booking data: as long as needed for scheduling and follow-up;
analytics data: according to the configured Google Analytics retention settings;
legal or compliance records: as long as required or reasonably necessary.
